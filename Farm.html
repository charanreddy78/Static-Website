Agriculture Tools in a Farmer’s Life?

Agriculture tools have an important role in every farmer’s life as these tools provide efficiency, productivity and other benefits. It also helps in improving soil health and precision farming. We can say agriculture tools are the backbone of this modern agriculture era as they promote sustainable growth in the agricultural sector.

Machinery and tools invented to reduce human effort. It is evident that all the farmers want to increase their production in the field with minimum effort. These tools and machinery used interchangeably. The considerable knowledge of tools can unlock the chances of success. These agriculture tools used primarily for weed elimination, fertilizer, general tillage, elimination, and fumigation. To take these points in mind, we come up with a list of Farm tools names and tools used in agriculture. Check out below.

Plow Animal Ox

Indian farmers ploughing their fields manually using oxen at a village in Southern India. They also use ox cart to transport their crops from one place to another. Traditional ploughing of farms by using iron ploughs and bulls was one of the major professions of farmers in almost all villages in the near past. But the advent of modern ploughing machines has forced the traditional ploughing method into dark.

Tractor

Tractors can be used for ploughing, tilling, harrowing, sowing, and harvesting. They can also be used for hauling heavy loads, landscaping tasks, and more. With their constant innovation and development, tractors have made farming a more efficient and profitable enterprise for farmers in India

Harvester

Combine harvesters, also known as harvesters, are agricultural machines designed to efficiently harvest, thresh, and winnow crops. Here are the primary uses of harvesters: Harvesting: Cut and collect crops such as wheat, rice, oats, barley, corn, soybeans, sunflowers, and pulses. Threshing: Separate the edible grain from the inedible chaff and straw. Winnowing: Remove impurities and excess chaff from the grain, leaving a clean and dry product.

Sprayer

Agricultural sprayers are essential equipment in modern farming, used to apply herbicides, pesticides, fertilizers, and other crop management products to crops. These devices ensure efficient and targeted application of chemicals, promoting healthy plant growth and reducing environmental impact. Types of Agricultural Sprayers Manual Sprayers: Portable, handheld devices suitable for small-scale farming or garden applications. Backpack Sprayers: Wearable units with a reservoir and pump, ideal for small to medium-sized farms.

Insecticide

Insecticides are pesticides used to kill insects.[1] They include ovicides and larvicides used against insect eggs and larvae, respectively. Acaricides, which kill mites and ticks, are not strictly insecticides, but are usually classified together with insecticides. The major use of Insecticides is agriculture, but they are also used in home and garden, industrial buildings, vector control and control of insect parasites of animals and humans. Insecticides are claimed to be a major factor behind the increase in the 20th-century's agricultural productivity.

Drone

Drones are one of the most important modern agricultural tools. It is an unmanned aerial vehicle that helps in yield optimization, monitoring crop growth and thereby increasing production. Working They typically work as other modes of air transportation, when the engine is turned on ,it starts up and the propellers rotate to enable flight. They are controlled by remote operators.This is one of the important tools used in agriculture.
